Our background

An Irish based rock band making big moves in the local scene. The collection features Jamie Ahern, Arthur Adams and Frank Collins from county Limerick. Afterlife have been selling out venues and rocking crowds all across the country since 2019. Between high energy bangers and slow jams, they are sure to have a sound to fit everyones taste.
